HOW WE WORK

Tell us who you are and what you want ?

Our aim is to use algorithms to match you with the right opportunity. Our members are looking for their own career success story and we do everything to help them achieve it.

At connections, all you have to do is create your profile. You can find matches using the criteria search. Our partners are organised according to the type of employment that they offer. Describe your ideal role/job in one sentence. Detail the skills that you have. If you like their profile and they like yours we will automatically connect you. A chat window will open and they will have access to your full CV.

We have an online virtual career coach to help you during every step of the way
